Chemical Peel

Chemical peel treatments improve skin texture by applying a specially formulated solution to the face. This solution is customized to achieve the desired level of exfoliation and is left on the skin for several minutes or longer, depending on the peel ’s intensity.

Read More

Q Switched ND Yag Laser

Laser stands for ‘light amplification by stimulated emission of radiation.’Dermatologists use Q-switch lasers for various procedures. These lasers emit high-intensity light with a single wavelength (one color) and are unidirectional, targeting specific tissues in the skin known as chromophores.

Read More

CO2 Laser

The carbon dioxide (CO2) laser generates a specific wavelength of light in the mid-infrared spectrum, which allows its energy to be effectively absorbed by the water in the skin. This absorption capability makes it highly effective for precise and controlled skin treatments.
Read More

Intense Pulsed Light (IPL)

Intense Pulsed Light (IPL) is a non-invasive treatment that uses high-intensity pulses of visible light to improve skin appearance. It effectively addresses a variety of skin concerns, including mild to moderate acne scars, hyperpigmentation (brown spots), wrinkles, large pores, excessive flushing, age spots, sunspots, broken capillaries, spider veins, and stretch marks.
Read More

Diode Laser Hair Removal

Diode laser hair removal offers a solution to your ongoing efforts to maintain smooth, hair-free skin. This cosmetic medical procedure uses a pulsating beam of light from a laser machine to target and destroy hair follicles. The light energy is absorbed by the pigment in the hair, effectively and precisely removing unwanted hair.

Meet The Best Dermatologists In Pune

Clear Skin and HairMD started in 1989 as the brainchild of Dr Dhananjay Chavan in the small town of Karad, Maharashtra. Dr Chavan realised that Acne, Pigmentation, Skin Care and Hair loss treatments were often looked at as luxuries and inaccessible in India and patients resorted to using home remedies or OTC products which were largely ineffective and frustrating.

They realised that people were looking for dermatologically approved evidence-based treatments at affordable prices. Dr Chavan started Clear Skin and HairMD with a team of 5, initially focussing on Acne and Hair Loss.Clear Skin Pune Station Procedure Room

In 2012, Clear Skin and HairMD opened its first clinic in Pune and soon after, it expanded to the rest of Pune &Pimpri Chinchwad. The name Clear Skin has now become synonymous with safe, modern, effective and time-tested medical treatments for all kinds of skin and cosmetic treatments. From an Original Team of 5, the Clear Skin family is now 100+ strong.
